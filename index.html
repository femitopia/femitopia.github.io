About Zipolite

Zipolite, located in Oaxaca, Mexico, is known for its peaceful atmosphere and nudist-friendly beach. Its 1.75 km of sandy coastline is perfect for swimming and surfing. Enjoy the consistent tropical weather while exploring nearby beaches like Estacahuite, La Mina, and La Boquilla. Discover charming towns such as San Agustinillo and Mazunte, home to the Mexican Turtle Center, or visit Huatulco Bay and Puerto Escondido for more adventures. Wildlife enthusiasts can observe armadillos, iguanas, dolphins, and migrating whales in the area.

Bring your true self to our space. We're a community where everyone is open, honest, and ready to grow. If you love nature, being by the ocean, and feel comfortable in sex-positive, nudist spaces, you'll fit right in. Know your community, respect pronouns, bring your skills or be ready to learn, and keep an open heart. Together, we'll create something special.

COME STAY WITH US!

When you book with us, we'll ask if you'd like to swap homes. We promise to care for your place and leave it just as you left it, or even better. Home swaps make travel more affordable and open up new destinations for our community worldwide.

Volunteer With Us & Help Build Our Dream

Volunteers are essential to our project, and we welcome everyone, regardless of financial means. We offer diverse opportunities, including permaculture, gardening, building, cooking, and more. Contact us with your skills to arrange a workshop or volunteering options.

Volunteers receive accommodation, food, and access to all workshops.

Discover Zipolite

A hidden gem on Mexico's Pacific Coast

Zipolite, located in Oaxaca, Mexico, is known for its peaceful atmosphere and nudist-friendly beach. Its 1.75 km of sandy coastline is perfect for swimming and surfing. Enjoy the consistent tropical weather. Visit charming towns like Mazunte, or head to Huatulco Bay and Puerto Escondido for more adventures. Spot wildlife such as dolphins, and migrating whales.

Our Community

Bring your true self to our space. We're a community where everyone is open, honest, and ready to grow. If you love nature, being by the ocean, and feel comfortable in sex-positive, nudist spaces, you'll fit right in.

Know your community, respect pronouns, bring your skills or be ready to learn, and keep an open heart. Together, we'll create something special.
